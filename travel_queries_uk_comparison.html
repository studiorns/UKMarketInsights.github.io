UK Travel Queries Forecast Comparison

This dashboard compares the original forecast with a more conservative forecast for UK travel queries in 2025.

Parameter Adjustments in Conservative Forecast

  • Base Growth Factor: Reduced from (5%, 10%, 15%) to (3%, 5%, 8%)
  • Media Effectiveness Multiplier: Reduced from (0.10, 0.15, 0.20) to (0.08, 0.12, 0.15)
  • Flight Search Correlation: Significantly reduced from (0.05, 0.10, 0.15) to (0.02, 0.04, 0.06) to temper the impact of high flight search growth

Key Insights from Comparison

More Realistic Growth Projections

The adjusted forecast provides more realistic growth projections, with the Conservative scenario showing 22.1% YoY growth compared to 46.3% in the original forecast. This is more in line with typical market growth rates and closer to the France market's growth projections.

Reduced Impact of Flight Search Growth

The original forecast was heavily influenced by the substantial growth in flight searches from 2023 to 2024. The adjusted forecast reduces the correlation between flight search growth and query growth to provide a more balanced projection.

Seasonality Patterns Maintained

Both forecasts maintain the same seasonality patterns, with January, February, March, September, and October showing the highest growth potential. This consistency reflects the underlying seasonal trends in the UK market.

Comparison to France Market

The adjusted forecast brings the UK market growth projections closer to the France market (2.3% to 30.6%), while still reflecting the UK's stronger growth potential due to higher flight search growth and effective media planning.

Brand Health Considerations

Both forecasts maintain the same brand health metrics definitions and multipliers, as the UK market already has strong brand consideration (33.09%) with modest targets for growth in 2025.

Brand Health Metrics Definitions:

  • Awareness: Percentage of people from the UK market who are aware of Abu Dhabi
  • Consideration: Percentage of people who consider traveling to Abu Dhabi in the next 3 years
  • Intent: Percentage of people who consider traveling to Abu Dhabi in the next 12 months
